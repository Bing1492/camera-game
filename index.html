Motion Catcher

This is a single-file browser game made for Android tablets and regular browsers. Your camera becomes the controller. Move in the left, center, or right side of the camera view to move your ship to that lane and catch glowing orbs.

How to play

  • Tap Start camera and allow camera permission.
  • Stand or sit where the camera can clearly see you.
  • Wave a hand or move your head in one of the three screen zones.
  • Your ship jumps to that lane.
  • Catch the glowing orbs. Avoid the red blockers.
  • The game gets faster as your score goes up.

Android tablet tips

  • Open the file in Chrome on Android.
  • Use HTTPS or localhost. Camera access usually will not work from an insecure page.
  • Good lighting helps a lot.
  • If front camera is blocked, switch to the back camera.
  • If motion feels weak, move closer or make bigger gestures.
